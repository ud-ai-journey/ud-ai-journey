This allows you to prioritize your responses, manage your tasks, and communicate more effectively.

How to Use MailMind

Step 1: Input Your Email

You have two easy ways to provide the email content:

  1. Paste Content: Simply copy the text from your email client and paste it directly into the text area on the main page.
  2. Upload a File: Alternatively, you can save your email as a .txt, .eml, or .msg file and upload it using the file uploader.

Step 2: Analyze

Once your email content is in the app, click the large üöÄ Analyze Email button. The AI will process the text, which usually takes just a few seconds.

Step 3: Review the Results

The results are presented in three main cards:

  • Smart Summary: A condensed version of your email.
  • Action Items: A checklist of tasks identified in the email.
  • Tone Analysis: A description of the email's sentiment and character.

You can use the buttons below each card to copy the content or share it.

Step 4: Use the Sidebar

The sidebar is your command center. Here you can:

  • View your personal analytics dashboard.
  • Adjust settings for future analyses.
  • Clear your history or export your analytics data.
  • See a quick log of your most recent activity.
